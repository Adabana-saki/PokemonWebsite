Introducing the list of all-time Pokémon games in order!

The popular game series "Pocket Monsters (Pokémon)" presented by Nintendo announced the release of the latest series "Pokémon Scarlet & Violet" in the winter of 2022.
From its release in 1996 to the present, we will introduce a list of successive Pokémon games that have evolved and passed through various game hardware and still boast unabated popularity!
We will post the release dates of the Pokémon game series so far, successive generations, suggested retail prices, sales pages, and game overviews in order of release date, divided by generation!
With the release of the latest work "Pokémon Scarlet & Violet", how about replaying the series so far!
