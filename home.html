What is Pokémon

The "Pokémon" series is a role-playing game (RPG) in which the player becomes the main character and goes on an adventure with mysterious creatures called pocket monsters (Pokémon for short)!
One of the major features of this work is that you can not only enjoy the story alone, but also enjoy it with your friends by exchanging Pokemon and fighting.

Starter Pokémon

The adventure of "Pokémon" begins when you choose one of the three Pokémon to be your partner and go on a journey as a Pokémon Trainer!

  • Sprigatito

  • Feucoco

  • Quaxly

In "Pokémon Scarlet & Violet", you will choose your first partner from Sprigatito, Feucoco and Quaxly! As a partner to spend a long adventure together, it will be a very painful and memorable decision!"

Encounter with many people and Pokémon

During your adventure, many dramas await you, such as encounters with various people, encounters with legendary Pokemon, and confrontations with evil organizations!
While traveling through different adventure stages for each series, deepen your bond with Pokémon through various experiences, such as meeting many Pokémon Trainers, competing with Pokémon, finding and battling wild Pokémon, and more!
